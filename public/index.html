this garden - garden journal app

Make the most of your time in the garden.

A typical gardener might only get 40 growing seasons. Whether you’re growing vegetables or cut flowers, fruits or shrubs, you’ll want to remember everything that happens in your garden. Keep a journal using thisgarden and take the guesswork out of it.

Everything in one place

Add every plant in your garden, with no limits. Whether they're old favourites or just this year's experiment, keep track of everything you sow, plant, pick and harvest. Build your knowledge and look back at everything you accomplish.

A complete history

Every time you spot something interesting, take a note. Seeing slug damage? You'll want to remember when they first appeared. Dahlia bulbs spent half of April under water? You'll know why they didn't look so great come July.

Grow your knowledge

Look back at last year to see what worked well and what needs adjustment. Did your broad bean seeds sit dormant from February to April before producing a crop over the next 6 weeks? Plant them later this year and use that precious space for something more likely to show a return.
